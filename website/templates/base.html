Our Key Programs

At New Restoration Plan—Malawi, we implement a diverse portfolio of sustainable development, advocacy, and humanitarian initiatives designed to build community resilience, promote human rights, and improve livelihoods across Malawi, targeting the root causes of systemic poverty through grassroots ecological restoration, essential public health campaigns, and dedicated social welfare for vulnerable children, senior citizens, and persons with disabilities.

Our Partners & Networks

Our impact across Malawi is amplified through strategic collaborations with local leadership, regulatory authorities, and dedicated civil society networks.

🏛️

Institutional Partners

Collaborating closely with urban entities like the Lilongwe City Council (LCC) on comprehensive 5-year frameworks like the Mzinda Uwale project to realize cleaner environments matching global standards.

🌍

Grassroots Leaders

Partnering seamlessly alongside traditional leaders, including the Chewa Supreme Council representatives, ensuring all localized action points respect culture while delivering impactful changes.

🤝

Advocacy & Welfare Allies

Working reliably with strategic public health setups and safety nets safeguarding elderly citizens, youth networks, and persons with physical disabilities.
